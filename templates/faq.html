Frequently Questioned Answers

What is this?

Pinboard is a bookmarking and bookmark organization service.

How do I add a bookmark?

Though you can add them interactively here, the site is designed to be used primarily through bookmarklets.

Here are the bookmarklets offered:

  • Pin This - Submit current page to your pinboard
  • My Pinboard - Link to your pinboard
  • Reading List - Link to your read later list
  • Read Later - Submit current link to readlater
